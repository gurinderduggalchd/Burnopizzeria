Welcome to Burno's Pizzeria!

At Burno's Pizzeria, we believe that pizza is more than just a food—it's an art form. From our humble beginnings in 1965, we have been dedicated to perfecting the craft of pizza making and delivering an exceptional culinary experience to our customers.

Whether you're seeking a casual family dinner, a gathering with friends, or a place to celebrate a special occasion, Burno's Pizzeria provides a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can savor the art of pizza perfection. Our dedicated team of pizzaiolos ensures that every pizza is made with love and precision, delivering a taste experience that will keep you coming back for more.

Thank you for choosing Burno's Pizzeria. We invite you to join us on this delicious journey and indulge in the flavors that have made us a pizza institution.
